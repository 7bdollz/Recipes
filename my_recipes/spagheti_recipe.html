Spaghetti recipe

our recipe for making that delicious spaghetti hangon and tighten your seatbelt

Ingredients

  • 400 gram spaghetti (1 pack)
  • 4-5 medium fresh tomatoes (or 1 can chopped tin tomatoes)
  • 2 medium red bell peppers (tatashe)
  • 1-2 scotch bonnet peppers (adjust to taste)
  • 2 medium oinions (1 for blending,1 for sauteing)
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic (optional)
  • 1-inch piece ginger (optional)
  • 1/4 cups vegetabl oil (or palm oil)
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 2-3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2-3 cubes (maggi or knorr)
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1-2 bay leafs
  • 1-2 cups of chicken/beef stock
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots,green beans or peas (optional)
  • 1 cup pre-cooked chicken or beef or chrimp or fish (optional)
  • 1 cup of cana

steps

  1. Prepare the pepper blend:

    2. Blend the red bell peppers,scotch bonnet peppers,1 onion,garlic,and ginger into a smooth paste,set aside.
    if using canned tomatoes ,blend only the bell peppers ,scotch bonnet,onion, garlic, and ginger, then mix with the canned tomatoes.

  2. Parboil the spaghetti:

    3. Bring a large pot of water to a boil, add a pinch of salt, and cook the spaghetti for 3-5 mins (until slightly soften but not fully cooked).
    drain and set aside .(parboiling prevents the spaghetti from becomming mushy in the sauce.)

  3. Cook the sauce base:

    4. Heat 1/4 cup of vegetable oil (or palm oil )in a large pot or deep pan over medium heat.
    Dice the remaining onion and saute in the hot oil until translucent (about 2-3 mins).
    Add the tomatoe paste and fry for about 2-3 mins ,stirring constantly to prevent burning.this enhances the flavor color

  4. Add the pepper blend:

    5. Pour the blended tomato mixture into the pot.stir well. add curry powder,thyme ,bay leaves (if using),and stock cubes .
    stir and let the mixture cook for 10-15 mins on medium heat,stirring occasionally until the stew thickens and oil starts to separate(the sauce should lose its raw taste).
    if the sauce is too thick add a little stock or water.

  5. Season the sauce:

    6. taste the sauce add salt as needed.adjust the seasoning to preffered taste(you can add more stock cubes or extra pepper for extra flavour)
    if using vegetables (carrots,green beans,peas,) or proteins(chicken,shrimp,beef) add them now and stir.let simmer for 2-3 mins

  6. Cook the spaghettiin the sauce:

    7. Add 1 -2 cups of chicken/beef stock or water to the sauce depending on how saucy you want the dish.Bring to a simmer.
    add the Parboiled spaghettito the pot ,stirring gently to to coat it in the sauce. reduce the heat to low ,cover the pot
    and let the spaghetti cook for 10-15 mins,stirring occasionally to prevent sticking .add more stock or water little at a time
    if the sauce dries up before the spaghetti is cooked. the spaghettiis done when its tender and have absorbed the sauce,with a slightly thick flavourfull coating.

  7. Check and adjust:

    8. Taste the spaghetti and adjust seasoning if needed. remove bay leaves if used and turn of the heat.

  8. serve:

    9. serve hot as it is or with a side of fried pantain,boiled egg or grilled chicken and fish .
    garnish with chopped parsley for a fresh touch

Enjoy
